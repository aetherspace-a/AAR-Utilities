Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about integrating Asiana Utilities.

How do I connect the bot to my VAMS?
To sync your Virtual Airline Management System, use the configuration command setup by the administrator. Ensure your API keys are securely placed in the environment variables before initializing the sync module.
Does the bot run 24/7?
Yes, Asiana Utilities is hosted on a high-performance cloud instance, kept alive by external Uptime monitors, ensuring absolute zero downtime during your long-haul operations.
Can anyone use the /openserver command?
No. Critical operational commands like `/openserver` and `/purge` are restricted to users with `Manage Messages` permissions or specific Dispatcher roles assigned in your server settings.
How does the Voice Audio work?
The bot utilizes Discord's voice API. When an authorized user executes `/boarding` or `/safetyvideo`, the bot will join the user's current voice channel and stream high-quality pre-recorded MP3 files.
